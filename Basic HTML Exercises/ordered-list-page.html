Baking a Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to the required temperature.
  2. Gather and measure all ingredients.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Cream butter and sugar, then add eggs and mix well.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients, then pour the batter into a greased pan.
  6. Bake for the specified time until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let the cake cool before serving or decorating.
