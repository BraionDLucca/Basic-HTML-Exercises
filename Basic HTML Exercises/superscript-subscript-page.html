The chemical equation representing the formation of water from its constituent elements, hydrogen and oxygen, is given by: 2H2 + O2 = 2H2O
This equation indicates that two molecules of diatomic hydrogen (H2) react with one molecule of diatomic oxygen (O2) to produce two molecules of water (H2O).
